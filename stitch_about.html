Our Legacy

A Journey of Industrial Innovation

Sarl Ferdjioua Plastique et Services began as a vision to revolutionize the local plastics market. From a modest workshop in 1998, we have evolved into a national leader in high-performance plastic solutions, serving sectors ranging from food packaging to heavy industry.

Our growth is rooted in the belief that quality is not a destination but a continuous pursuit. By investing in the latest European manufacturing technologies and fostering a culture of technical excellence, we provide our clients with products that meet the world's most stringent standards.
